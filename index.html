Marvel Studios: Movies of Phase 4 (Review + Info)

Now that Black Panther: Wakanda Forever has hit the theaters all around the world, this marks the end of Marvel Studios' phase 4 - regarding movies. There had been a total of 7 movies starting from Black Widow (2021) and ending with Wakanda Forever, and throughout, Mavel has introduced many new characters such as America Chavez, the Eternals, Shang Chi, Namor & the Mighty Thor. Generally, movies of phase 4 haven't been favorable among the fans, many criticizing the “degrade in quality” from earlier movies such as Avengers: Infinity War & Endgame. However, I quite enjoyed most of these movies, and if you look at this phase on its own and as an introductory phase, you would enjoy it too.
