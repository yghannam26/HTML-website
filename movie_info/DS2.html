Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness

General Info:

  • Release date: May 6, 2022
  • Directed by: Sam Raimi
  • IMDb: 6.9/10
  • Budget: 200 million USD
  • Box Office: 955.7 million USD

Plot:

Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness is the second installment of the Doctor Strange franchise. It picks up right after where Wandavision (TV series) had ended with Wanda reading the darkhold. The movie starts with a brand new character - America Chavez - and a variant of Doctor Strange being chased by a demonic creature. The movie then quickly picks up from there to introducing the true villain of the movie, the scarlet witch, who wants to take hold of America Chavez's powers to find her two kids, Billy & Tommy, in other universes. Then throughout the movie, Doctor Strange with America Chavez by his side and the scarlet witch play a game of cat and mouse until eventually the scarlet witch yields and destroys every copy there is of the darkhold.
