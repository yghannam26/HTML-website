Plot:

Eternals introduces a new team of heroes to the MCU with a very interesting backstory and a somewhat dark background that stretches way back to the beginning of time. It also presents the celestials for the first time in the MCU; the celestials are those enormous cosmic beings who, among many tasks, create immortal beings (including the Eternals), and deviant creatures. The movie starts with the Eternals fighting with the last couple of deviants thousands of years ago before dispersing into different locations in the universe. But after the deviants return, the Eternals have to reassemble again to stop them, but in the process, they learn of their dark history and of a world-ending event. That causes tension among them and leads them to break apart into two teams, those who support the celestials and those who want to challenge them.
