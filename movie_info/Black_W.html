Black Widow

General Info:

  • Release date: July 9, 2021
  • Directed by: Cate Shortland
  • IMDb: 6.7/10
  • Budget: 200 million USD
  • Box Office: 379.8 million USD

Plot:

The movie takes place during the period between Civil War & Infinity War. It visits Natasha her sister's, Yelena Belova, rough childhood and how they were separated and then dragged to be a part of the same secret organization, without them knowing. The movie expands more on their relationship as sisters who haven't seen each other for a very long time but had met each other again in circumstances they had never thought they would never be in. The movie officially "passes the torch" to Yelena Belova as the new black widow.
