Shang-Chi & the Legend of the Ten Rings

General Info:

  • Release date: September 2, 2021
  • Directed by: Destin Daniel Cretton
  • IMDb: 7.4/10
  • Budget: 150-200 million USD
  • Box Office: 432.2 million USD

Plot:

This movie introduces Shang-Chi as the first Asian superhero in the MCU. It starts by showing his life before being a hero and his struggle with his relationship with his family; he wants stay away from his father; he believes that he's the reason behind his mother's death; and he hadn't seen his sister for so long. Then, after receiving a mysterious invitation from his father regarding his mother, he accepts it and decides to go on a risky journey that end up with him having to fight his father, who was being tricked by the true villain, the dweller in darkness, into hearing his wife speaking to him.
