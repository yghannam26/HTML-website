  • Budget: 200 million USD
  • Box Office: 1.916 billion USD

Plot:

Spider Man: No Way Home is the third installment of the franchise. It starts right where the previous movie (Far From Home) ended, when Mysterio had accused spiderman of killing him and then went to unveil his true identity to the world. The movie then shows how Peter Parker and his friends are handling the aftermath of the revelation, leading him to visit a fellow Avenger - Doctor Strange - in order to fix the situation. However, the situation only gets worse as a spell goes sideways, opening a “multiversal” gateway allowing anyone who has ever known a Peter Parker to enter it - even the villans!
