Black Panther: Wakanda Forever

General Info:

  • Release date: November 11, 2022
  • Directed by: Ryan Coogler
  • IMDb: 7.3/10
  • Budget: 250 million USD
  • Box Office: 425.8 million USD (as of 18/11/2022)

Plot:

“Wakanda Forever” is the sequel to the cultural phenomenon Black Panther. But now, not only does it focus on the cultural aspect, it only handles the death of King T'Challa (and the late actor Chadwick Boseman). It starts by showing how T'Challa passed away and how the severely affected the people of Wakanda, especially his family, are dealing with his passing and how they're somewhat still healing. The movie then introduces the “villain”, Namor, and his underwater kingdom Talocan who end up conflicting with the Wakandas, leading to Namor attacking and flooding Wakanda.
