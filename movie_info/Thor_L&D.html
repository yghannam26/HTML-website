Plot:

"Love & Thunder" is the fourth Thor movie. It takes place after the events of Endgame, at the end of which we see Thor and the guardians of the galaxy leave on a spaceship together. The movie starts off with them going on different adventures and fighting some bad guys, and then the usual Thor crew (Thor, Valkyrie, and Korg) takes over plus a new member, the mighty Thor (Jane), joins them. The four of them have to fight a deadly villain, Gorr, who's planning to kill of the gods for not hearing his prayers. They travel to Omnipotence City seeking the help of Zues, who refuses to help them, but end up leaving with his lighting bolt and fighting Gorr alone.
